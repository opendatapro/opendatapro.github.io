Privacy policy

The hosting provider may collect information such as the IP, browser type, language preference, referring site, date and time of each request, etc.

Google Analytics is used to collect data about visitor behaviour, such as what pages are visited, the number of visits, how long people stay on a particular site, etc.

If you choose to subscribe to the e-mail newsletter additional information is stored with our e-mail newsletter software provider, such as your name, email, etc.

This privacy policy is updated when neccessary and it is your responsibility to keep up. That is why it is recommended you frequently check this page for any changes.

Cookies

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitors computer, and that the visitors browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns.

Usage

Cookies are used to:

  • collect analytics data, such as what pages on the website visitors visit, how long they spend on a particular site, etc.
  • offer a way of opting out of collecting analytics data
  • offer additional functionality, such as not displaying notifications or promotions to users over and over again.
